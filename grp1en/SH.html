Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a safety concern?

You can report safety concerns by contacting any teacher directly, using our anonymous reporting form, or speaking with Counselor Maria during office hours. All reports are taken seriously and handled with confidentiality.

What should I do if I feel uncomfortable in a situation?

Trust your instincts. Remove yourself from the situation if possible, and then tell a trusted adult immediately. Remember our safety mantra: "No, Go, Tell" - Say no, remove yourself from the situation, and tell a trusted adult.

How can I join the peer support group?

Our peer support group meets every Thursday at 4 PM. You can join by simply showing up to the meeting, or you can email Counselor Maria to let her know you're interested. There's no commitment - come once or come every week!

Are the counseling sessions confidential?

Yes, with some important exceptions. What you share with our counselors is confidential, unless there's concern about someone's safety (including your own), or if required by law. Your counselor will explain confidentiality fully during your first session.
