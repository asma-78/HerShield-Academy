Community Guidelines

Our guidelines help create a safe, respectful, and supportive environment for everyone.

Online Safety

  • Keep personal information private
  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Be cautious with links and downloads
  • Report suspicious activity immediately
  • Use privacy settings appropriately

Respectful Communication

  • Use inclusive language
  • Respect different opinions
  • No bullying or harassment
  • Be mindful of tone in text
  • Practice active listening

Session Guidelines

  • Join sessions on time
  • Mute when not speaking
  • Respect others' speaking time
  • Use appropriate backgrounds
  • Dress appropriately for sessions
