Safety Planning Tips

Emergency Contacts

Keep important numbers programmed in your phone and written down in case your phone is unavailable.

Safe Locations

Identify safe places you can go in an emergency, like friends' houses, family homes, or public places.

Digital Safety

Clear your browser history after visiting help sites and use private browsing mode when seeking help online.

Emergency Bag

Keep a bag with essentials (ID, money, medications, important documents) in case you need to leave quickly.
